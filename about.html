About us

Circular Economy Alliance aims to bring together like-minded organizations that have the vision and means to continually generate skill and knowledge to keenly support the adoption of circular economy practices across all pillars of societies.

Vision

Be the epicenter of Circular Economy where authorities advocates and practitioners, partner for the proliferation and distribution of knowledge and the upskilling of people, making them agents of change towards a sustainable future.

Mission

Provide people with comprehensive, accredited, reliable, credible, skills and knowledge on the circular economy spectrum.
